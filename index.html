Some interesting resources to learn the circle method from are:

  1. Analytic Methods for Diophantine Equations and Diophantine Inequalities by Harold Davenport. I have found this beautifully written.
  2. The Hardy-Littlewood method, by R. C. Vaughan . This is probably one of the most useful references on the subject.
